About Us -Overview

Transforming Rural Maharashtra Since 1991

Our Journey

Sadhana Shikshan Mandal (SSM) was founded in 1991 by Sharad Shinde, an Electronics Engineer from Manipal Institute of Technology (1985). Despite opportunities to work with multinational companies like his peers, Sharad chose a different path—one of service and community transformation.

As a former trustee of the renowned Sant Gajanan Maharaj Sansthan in Shegaon (known nationwide for discipline, charity, and integrity), Sharad witnessed the urgent need for affordable education and holistic development in rural Maharashtra. This experience shaped SSM's mission: to create self-reliant communities through education, skills, sustainable livelihoods, and values-based development.

For over three decades, SSM has remained committed to this vision, working at the grassroots level in Western Maharashtra's most underserved areas. We don't just run programs—we build partnerships with communities, understanding their unique challenges and co-creating sustainable solutions.

Founder & Team

Our Founder & Chairman

Hon. Shri Sharad Shinde

At Sadhana Shikshan Mandal, our journey has always been guided by a simple but powerful belief, that true development begins when communities are empowered to shape their own futures. Working closely with rural communities in and around Shegaon and the Buldhana region, we have witnessed how challenges in education, agriculture, health, women’s participation, and youth opportunities are deeply interconnected.

Addressing them in isolation offers only temporary relief. Our vision, therefore, is to build an integrated ecosystem of education, innovation, health, and sustainable livelihoods that enables long-term self-reliance. Our initiatives across education, women empowerment, entrepreneurship, sustainable farming, health support, and sports development are designed not merely as welfare interventions, but as capacity-building platforms.

By combining grassroots understanding with technology-driven solutions and strong partnerships, we aim to create impact that is measurable, scalable, and enduring. We take pride in our values of discipline, transparency, and service, inspired by our institutional roots and reinforced through collaborations with government agencies, academic institutions, and responsible corporate partners.

Success Story

Soya to Soya Paneer –
Empowering Women in Gaulkhed

In 2022, two determined women from the small village of Gaulkhed decided to transform a simple idea into a sustainable source of income. With the guidance and mentorship of Monali Madam and Dr. Santosh Bothe, and the continuous support of SSM’s Women Empowerment Program, they learned how to process soya into nutritious soya paneer right from their homes.

Starting with minimal resources, they partnered with a local vegetable vendor who helped them reach nearby households and village markets. Through persistence and teamwork, they gradually turned their home-based venture into a reliable source of income. Today, these women earn enough to support their families with dignity.

“With SSM’s support and the guidance of Monali Madam and Dr. Bothe, we turned soya into self-reliance — our kitchen became our workplace, and our work gave us pride.”

Sustainable Farming & Rural Development

Transforming Agricultural Practices

We advance rural prosperity through ecological balance and economic resilience. Our agricultural programs promote the adoption of climate-smart, natural farming techniques to enhance farm productivity while protecting the environment.

By integrating agri-tech solutions, facilitating farmer-producer collectives, and creating sustainable market linkages, we work to increase incomes, reduce carbon footprints, and build regenerative food systems - essentially creating a local circular economy. This integrated model strengthens the entire rural economy, ensuring long-term food security and environmental sustainability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find Answers to Common Questions About Our Work and How You Can Get Involved

What is Sadhana Shikshan Mandal's main focus area?

Sadhana Shikshan Mandal focuses on integrated rural development through six key pillars: Education, Women Empowerment, Entrepreneurship & Skill Building, Sustainable Farming & Rural Development, Sports Development, and Health Support. Our holistic approach addresses multiple dimensions of rural poverty to create sustainable impact.

How can I donate to SSM?

You can donate to SSM through multiple channels:

  • Online donation through our secure payment gateway on the website
  • Bank transfer to our account (details available on request)
  • Cheque/DD in favor of "Sadhana Shikshan Mandal"
  • UPI payment using our UPI ID: donate@sadhana

All donations are eligible for 50% tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act.

How does SSM ensure transparency in fund utilization?

SSM maintains complete transparency through:

  • Annual financial audit by certified chartered accountants
  • Regular program monitoring and evaluation reports
  • Detailed impact assessment for all major programs
  • Public disclosure of financial statements on our website
  • Donor-specific reporting for significant contributions
  • Compliance with all regulatory requirements including 12A, 80G, and CSR-1

Can corporations partner with SSM for CSR initiatives?

Yes, SSM is CSR-1 registered and actively partners with corporations for their CSR initiatives. We offer:

  • Customized project design aligned with corporate CSR focus areas
  • End-to-end project implementation and management
  • Regular monitoring and transparent reporting
  • Impact assessment and documentation
  • Employee engagement opportunities
  • Brand visibility through program association

We have successful partnerships with several leading corporations across various sectors.

How can I volunteer with SSM?

We welcome volunteers with diverse skills and backgrounds. You can:

  • Fill out the volunteer application form on our website
  • Choose from various opportunities including teaching, field support, content creation, digital skills training, and more
  • Select time commitment that suits you - from a few hours weekly to full-time immersion
  • Volunteer remotely or on-site based on the opportunity

Our volunteer coordinator will contact you to discuss suitable opportunities based on your skills and interests.

How does SSM measure impact?

SSM employs a comprehensive monitoring and evaluation framework:

  • Baseline surveys before program implementation
  • Regular progress monitoring against predefined indicators
